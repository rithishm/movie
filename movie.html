The Wolverine
Haunted by his past and burdened by immortality, Logan travels to Japan where he faces his ultimate nemesis.

“The Wolverine” blends samurai honor with mutant power, pushing our hero to his physical and emotional limits.

This is the story of pain, loss, and redemption — the man behind the claws.
Deadpool
Wade Wilson is a former special forces operative turned mercenary with a twisted sense of humor and a healing factor to match.

“Deadpool” is a wild, irreverent ride through breaking the fourth wall, hilarious violence, and unconventional heroics.

With great power comes zero responsibility.
